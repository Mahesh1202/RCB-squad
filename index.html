RCB Players Profile

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who plays Test and ODI cricket and is a former captain. He is a right-handed batsman known as the King and the Chase Master.

Phil Salt

Philip Salt is a Welsh cricketer who plays for England and Lancashire, known as an aggressive right-handed opener and occasional wicketkeeper.

Devdutt Padikkal

Devdutt Padikkal is an Indian cricketer playing for Karnataka and has represented RCB, Rajasthan Royals, and Lucknow Super Giants in IPL.

Rajat Patidar

Rajat Patidar is an Indian top-order batter and off-spinner who plays for Madhya Pradesh and captains RCB in IPL 2025.

Jitesh Sharma

Jitesh Sharma is an Indian cricketer who debuted internationally against Nepal during the 2022 Asian Games and plays for Vidarbha and RCB.

Krunal Pandya

Krunal Pandya is an Indian all-rounder who bats left-handed and bowls slow left-arm orthodox, playing for Baroda and RCB in IPL.

Tim David

Tim David is a Singaporean-Australian cricketer known for his big hitting and part of Australia's squad for the 2022 T20 World Cup.

Bhuvneshwar Kumar

Josh Hazlewood

Josh Hazlewood debuted with a bang during the 2014-15 home series against India, taking a five-wicket haul at the Gabba.

Yash Dayal

Yash Dayal impressed in his debut IPL season with Gujarat Titans, although he later faced tough moments against KKR's Rinku Singh.
